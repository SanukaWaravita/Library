# 02 - Business Requirements & SMART

## Overview

Business requirements define what the organization needs to achieve its strategic objectives. SMART criteria ensure requirements are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound, providing clear, actionable specifications.

## Business Requirements Fundamentals

### Definition and Scope
- **Strategic Alignment:** Link to organizational goals and objectives
- **Business Value:** Clear business benefits and outcomes
- **Stakeholder Focus:** Key business stakeholder needs
- **Outcome-Oriented:** Focus on results rather than solutions

### Characteristics
- **High-Level:** Broad organizational needs
- **Strategic:** Long-term business impact
- **Measurable:** Quantifiable business outcomes
- **Actionable:** Can be translated into system requirements

## SMART Criteria Framework

### Specific (S)
- **Clear Definition:** Exactly what needs to be accomplished
- **Who, What, Where, When:** Complete requirement context
- **Avoid Vagueness:** No ambiguous terms or generalities
- **Concrete Outcomes:** Specific results expected

**Examples:**
- ❌ "Improve customer service"
- ✅ "Reduce customer complaint resolution time from 48 hours to 24 hours"

### Measurable (M)
- **Quantifiable Metrics:** Clear measurement criteria
- **Success Indicators:** How success will be determined
- **Progress Tracking:** Ability to monitor achievement
- **Objective Standards:** Data-driven evaluation

**Examples:**
- ❌ "Increase sales"
- ✅ "Increase monthly sales by 15% within 6 months"

### Achievable (A)
- **Realistic Goals:** Attainable within constraints
- **Resource Availability:** Required resources accessible
- **Technical Feasibility:** Technology capabilities adequate
- **Organizational Capability:** Skills and capacity available

**Examples:**
- ❌ "Achieve 100% customer satisfaction"
- ✅ "Achieve 85% customer satisfaction rating"

### Relevant (R)
- **Business Alignment:** Supports organizational objectives
- **Value Proposition:** Clear business benefits
- **Stakeholder Impact:** Important to key stakeholders
- **Strategic Fit:** Aligns with business strategy

**Examples:**
- ❌ "Implement new CRM system"
- ✅ "Implement CRM system to reduce customer acquisition cost by 20%"

### Time-bound (T)
- **Deadline Specification:** Clear completion timeframe
- **Milestone Definition:** Intermediate checkpoints
- **Urgency Indication:** Time-sensitive requirements
- **Schedule Constraints:** Realistic timeline expectations

**Examples:**
- ❌ "Improve system performance"
- ✅ "Improve system response time to under 2 seconds by Q2 2026"

## Business Requirements Categories

### Strategic Requirements
- **Market Position:** Competitive advantage objectives
- **Growth Objectives:** Revenue and market expansion goals
- **Innovation Goals:** New product and service development
- **Risk Management:** Business risk mitigation objectives

### Operational Requirements
- **Process Efficiency:** Operational process improvement
- **Cost Reduction:** Expense reduction objectives
- **Quality Improvement:** Service and product quality goals
- **Compliance Requirements:** Regulatory and legal requirements

### Customer-Focused Requirements
- **Customer Experience:** Customer satisfaction objectives
- **Service Delivery:** Service quality and speed goals
- **Customer Retention:** Customer loyalty objectives
- **Market Expansion:** New customer acquisition goals

### Financial Requirements
- **Revenue Objectives:** Sales and income targets
- **Cost Management:** Expense control objectives
- **Profitability Goals:** Profit margin improvement
- **Investment Returns:** ROI and financial performance objectives

## Business Requirements Documentation

### Business Requirements Document (BRD)
- **Executive Summary:** Business case and objectives
- **Business Requirements:** Detailed SMART requirements
- **Stakeholder Analysis:** Key stakeholder identification
- **Success Metrics:** Measurement and success criteria

### Business Case
- **Problem Statement:** Business problem definition
- **Solution Options:** Alternative solution evaluation
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis:** Financial justification
- **Implementation Plan:** High-level implementation approach

### Requirement Traceability
- **Business Objective:** Link to strategic objectives
- **Functional Requirements:** Derived system requirements
- **Test Cases:** Associated validation criteria
- **Change History:** Requirement modification tracking

## SMART Requirements Best Practices

### Writing SMART Requirements
- **Collaborative Development:** Business and technical team collaboration
- **Stakeholder Involvement:** Key stakeholder participation
- **Iterative Refinement:** Progressive requirement improvement
- **Validation Focus:** Regular requirement validation

### Common Pitfalls
- **Vague Language:** Avoid ambiguous terms
- **Over-ambition:** Ensure realistic achievability
- **Missing Context:** Include complete requirement context
- **Ignoring Constraints:** Consider resource and time limitations

## Business Requirements Challenges

### Stakeholder Management
- **Diverse Perspectives:** Different stakeholder viewpoints
- **Political Issues:** Organizational politics impact
- **Communication Gaps:** Misunderstanding between groups
- **Expectation Management:** Realistic expectation setting

### Business Environment
- **Market Changes:** Rapid business condition changes
- **Competitive Pressures:** Competitive response requirements
- **Regulatory Changes:** Compliance requirement evolution
- **Technology Advances:** New technology opportunity consideration

### Organizational Issues
- **Resource Constraints:** Limited budget and personnel
- **Change Resistance:** Resistance to new requirements
- **Priority Conflicts:** Competing requirement prioritization
- **Scope Management:** Requirement scope control
